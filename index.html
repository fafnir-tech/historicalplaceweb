This is Cagsawa Ruins

also known as

The Cagsawa Ruins stand as a poignant reminder of nature's power and the resilience of the human spirit. Once a thriving town and church, the ruins now consist primarily of the iconic belfry, a testament to the devastating 1814 eruption of the Mayon Volcano. The ruins serve as a historical landmark and a symbol of both loss and endurance.
